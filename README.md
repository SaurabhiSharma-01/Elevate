# 🚀 Elevate

### *From Learning to Landing Your Dream Career.*

**Elevate** is an AI-powered Career Intelligence Platform that bridges the gap between **Students**, **Colleges**, and **Recruiters**. It transforms the traditional Training & Placement Cell into a smart ecosystem where students receive personalized career guidance, colleges gain data-driven insights, and companies discover job-ready talent.

---

## 📌 The Problem

Current placement systems focus mainly on job postings and resume collection.

* Students don't know their skill gaps or placement readiness.
* Colleges struggle to track and prepare students effectively.
* Recruiters spend significant time filtering candidates.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

Elevate uses Artificial Intelligence to guide students throughout their placement journey—from skill assessment to final interview preparation.

The platform consists of three integrated portals:

* 👨‍🎓 **Student Portal**
* 🏫 **College (Training & Placement Cell) Portal**
* 🏢 **Company Recruiter Portal**

---
